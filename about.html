About Zodhya

Zodhya is currently working on reducing energy consumption, lowering carbon footprint, and enhancing the overall way of using energy with our technology.


Founded by IIT Madras alumni & incubated at T-hub, Hyderabad, Zodhya is currently working on reducing energy consumption and lowering carbon footprint using technology. We believe that it's the small things that we do today that make a huge difference in our tomorrow. Our only goal is to make your day to day life better by contributing to the areas of energy and the environment.


So far we have saved about 50,000 electrical units which translate to 45,000 kg CO2 emissions reduced. We look to scale this reduction to 1 million kg emission reduction over the next 18 months through our technology.
